Visual Syllabus

A course requires a course outline (or syllabus). When we all taught in a class room, this information was printed out. To be handed out and read in class. The course outline contained all the important. It contained important information about policies, assignments and other key details

But now, with many parts of a course online, a course website with it's many different links. Is replacing the syllabus. There's still a need to offer a take away summary or birds eye view. Cue the visual syllabus. Which acts as an infographic for your course.

The visual syllabus is a simple collection of graphic elements, that allows for quick visual scan of the information. Its not a replacement for the long form syllabus. But rather. Its a take away summary that is easier to comprehend and take with you
